CommunityCall MN

January 2023

devpost.com/software/myideapublic

In response to the prompt: “create a project that encourages civil participation,” I drafted a forum-inspired project to encourage civic duties within a member's local city & community. Citizens can view petitions on their city board and also easily connect with local officials: removing some of the barriers surrounding the area where participation matters the most.

  • Original project developed over the Minnehack2023, competition-standard 24h time limit
  • Frontend developed using HTML & Tailwind
  • Backend developed using Flask (python)
  • Database was done in SQLite wrapped in SQLAlchemy with tables to hold petitions and city data

Cryptocurrency Mining

January 2020 - August 2022

Binder Investments LLC

In this venture, I Procured, Networked, and Constructed three cryptocurrency farm computers to produce Ethereum & Bitcoin. This sparked my passion for entrepreneurship, cryptocurrency, trading, finance, and software development. Additionally, I set up a personal coin alert & coin tracker to monitor vital crypto analytics and make better trades. This entire operation began in early 2017, and became more serious around 2020 when I upgraded from one to 3 cryptocurrency farm computers. Throughout the entire process, I made sure to constantly update the business plan where required and continue accounting important financial information until the eventual shutdown of Ethereum mining in late 2022.

  • Designed the business plan and researched the cryptocurrency ecosystem and market
  • Calculated CapEx & OpEx to accurately predict expenses
  • Managed failures, hardware, and software components remotely
  • Maintained an uptime of 99%
